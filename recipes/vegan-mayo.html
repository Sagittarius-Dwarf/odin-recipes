Vegan Mayo

Description

An unctious vegan mayo that will satisfy any carnivore.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Aquafaba
  • 3/4-1 Cup Neutral Oil
  • 1-3tsp Simple Syrup
  • 1/2tsp Mustard
  • 1/4tsp Salt
  • 1 1/2tsp White Vinegar

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients but oil.
  2. Slowly trickle in oil.
  3. Keep adding oil until emulsified.
