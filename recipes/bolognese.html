Bolognese

Descrption

My own take on a bolognese sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 Sticks of Celery
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Carrot
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 500g Mince Beef
  • 150g Bacon
  • 2 Cans of Diced Tomatoes
  • 20g Basil
  • 1 Beef Stock Cube
  • 2tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 1-2 Chillies
  • 1 Cup Red Wine
  • 100g Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Parmesan
  • 2tbsp Fish Sauce
  • 1tsp MSG
  • Salt to Taste

Steps

  1. Cut up bacon and render the fat slowly.
  2. Remove bacon bits but leave the fat in the pan.
  3. Add half of the butter.
  4. Add onion to the pan and cook until softened.
  5. Add minced garlic and chilli, sautee until fragrant.
  6. Add carrot and celery and sautee until softened.
  7. Keep tasting and adding salt as needed.
  8. Add tomato paste and caramelize.
  9. Add beef and cooked bacon.
  10. When the meat is browned, deglaze with red wine.
  11. Add broth and tomoatoes.
  12. Add fish sauce and MSG.
  13. Simmer for 1.5 hours.
  14. Taste and season as needed.
  15. Add milk and simmer for another 30 minutes.
  16. Add remaining butter, basil, and parmesan.
